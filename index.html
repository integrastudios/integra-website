What is Integra

Healing that works on every level — all at once.

Integra is a private practice offering Biofield Therapy — a deeply integrated approach to energy healing that combines Reiki, Seichem, Sound Healing and Meditation Therapy into one cohesive session.

Every session is channelled in the moment. Nothing is planned. Everything is felt. This is healing that honours both the science of the body and the wisdom of the soul.
