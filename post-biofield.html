What is the Biofield — and why does it matter for your healing?

The biofield is not an alternative concept — it is a measurable, scientifically documented aspect of human biology. Understanding it changes everything about how you think about healing.

Emma Fletcher · Integra Studios · Adelaide

What exactly is the biofield?

The term biofield was formally adopted by the National Institutes of Health in the 1990s to describe the complex of energetic fields that regulate biological processes. In simple terms: your body is not just flesh and bone. It is also an electromagnetic field — a living, dynamic system of energy that extends beyond the skin and responds to everything around it.

Research from institutions including the HeartMath Institute has demonstrated that the heart alone generates an electromagnetic field measurable several feet from the body. This field carries information. It communicates with the fields of others. It responds to emotional states in ways science can now measure.

"The human body is not a machine. It is a field of intelligence — and healing begins at the level of the field."

What does the research actually say?

A growing body of peer-reviewed research supports the existence and clinical relevance of the biofield. Studies have shown that experienced energy healers can influence biological processes — including cell growth, bacterial cultures, and nervous system regulation — through intentional interaction with the biofield.

Research published in scientific journals including the Journal of Alternative and Complementary Medicine has documented significant reductions in pain, anxiety and cortisol levels following energy healing interventions. These are not placebo effects. They are measurable physiological changes.

Scientists such as Kibret et al. (2014) have demonstrated that the human body itself can act as an antenna — a resonant structure capable of transmitting and receiving electromagnetic energy. This means our physical form is not simply biological, but also an energetic system in constant dialogue with the environment around it.

Why does this matter for healing?

Most conventional healthcare works at the level of the physical body — treating symptoms after they have already manifested as disease or dysfunction. Biofield therapy works upstream of that. It works at the level of the field that organises and informs the physical body.

This is why clients often describe shifts that are difficult to put into words — a lightness, a clarity, a sense of having been returned to themselves. These are not vague impressions. They are the results of working at the level where change is most fundamental.
