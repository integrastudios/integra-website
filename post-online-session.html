What to expect from an online Biofield Therapy session

Energy does not stop at a door. It does not require physical proximity to move, to shift, or to heal. Online Biofield Therapy sessions with Emma offer the same depth of channelled work as in-person — because the field that is being worked with has no edges.

Emma Fletcher · Integra Studios · Adelaide

Does distance healing actually work?

This is the question most people carry into their first online session, even if they do not say it aloud. It is a fair question — and it deserves a direct answer.

Energy healing works with the biofield, which is not confined to the physical body. Research in quantum biology and consciousness studies has demonstrated that energetic connections can be established and maintained across physical distance. Experienced practitioners have long worked remotely with results consistent with in-person sessions.

The mechanisms are not yet fully explained by conventional science — but neither are many aspects of consciousness, perception and healing that we accept without question. What is consistently reported by clients is this: it works. The session feels real, the shifts are real, and the integration that follows is real.

"I was honestly sceptical about whether it would feel different online. It did not — if anything, being in my own space made it easier to go deeper." — Online client, Melbourne

What you will need

An online session requires very little setup. You will need a quiet space where you will not be interrupted for 90 minutes — your bedroom, a comfortable corner of your home, anywhere that feels private and calm. Lie down on your bed or a sofa rather than sitting at a desk. The session is designed to be received in a horizontal, receptive position.

You will need a device with a camera and stable internet — a laptop or tablet works better than a phone for this. We will connect via video at the start of the session for our opening conversation, and Emma will guide you from there.

Headphones are recommended, particularly for the sound therapy component. While the frequencies of crystal bowls, chimes and tuning forks are delivered differently online than in person, headphones allow the sound to travel more directly into the body and create a more immersive experience.

Dim the lights if you can. Have a glass of water nearby. Turn your phone to silent.

How the session unfolds

The session opens with a video conversation — the same gentle, unhurried opening as an in-person session. You share what is present for you, what has been weighing on you, or simply that you arrived with an open mind and no agenda. Emma will guide you from there.

You will then lie down, close your eyes, and the session begins. Sound therapy is delivered through your speakers or headphones, carrying you into a state of deep receptivity. The energy work that follows is channelled remotely — Emma working with your field from her space as you rest in yours.

The session is fully interactive in the sense that Emma remains present throughout, channelling whatever arises. You may be guided through a meditation, offered a grounding visualisation, or simply held in silence as the energy moves. Everything is responsive to what your field presents in that moment.

What you might notice

Many clients report that online sessions feel surprisingly intimate — perhaps because being in your own space removes any unfamiliarity and allows you to settle more quickly. The physical sensations are often just as present: warmth, tingling, heaviness, the sense of something releasing.

The integration period — the hours and days following the session — tends to be similar to in-person work. Rest afterwards if you can. Drink water. Give yourself space rather than rushing back into demands.

Online sessions are available for clients across Australia. In-person sessions are available in Adelaide. Both formats use the same channelled approach — the same depth, the same presence, the same commitment to what is actually needed in that moment.

If you are curious about which format is right for you, simply book — and trust that wherever you are, the session will find what it needs to find.
