What is the Biofield — and why does it matter for your healing?

The biofield is not a mystical concept — it is a scientifically documented energetic field that surrounds and permeates the human body. Here's what the research actually says.

Emma Fletcher · Reiki Master HH Dip. (R.T)
← Back to Journal

When people hear the word "biofield," they often assume it belongs to the realm of alternative medicine — something unverifiable, unprovable, perhaps even invented. But the biofield is neither new nor unscientific. It is a term formally adopted by the National Institutes of Health in the 1990s to describe the complex of fields and forces that regulate biological processes.

In simple terms: your body is not just flesh and bone. It is also an electromagnetic field. A living, pulsing system of energy that extends beyond the skin and responds to everything — thought, emotion, environment, other people's fields.

"The body is not a machine. It is a field of intelligence — and healing begins at the level of the field."

What the science says

Research from institutions including the HeartMath Institute has demonstrated that the heart generates the body's most powerful electromagnetic field — one that can be measured several feet away from the body. This field carries information. It communicates with the fields of others. It responds to emotional states in measurable ways.

Sound healing works on a similar principle. When tuning forks or crystal bowls are sounded near the body, the frequencies they emit interact with the body's own electromagnetic field — a process called entrainment. The nervous system begins to synchronise with the external frequency, moving toward coherence, calm, and receptivity.

Why this matters for your healing

Most conventional healthcare works at the level of the physical body — treating symptoms after they've manifested. Biofield therapy works upstream of that. It works at the level of the field that organises and informs the physical body.

This is why clients often describe shifts that feel difficult to explain — a lightness, a clarity, a sense of having been returned to themselves. These are not placebo effects. They are the results of working at the level where change is most fundamental.

In my practice at Integra, every session combines Reiki, Seichem, sound healing and meditation therapy — not as separate treatments layered together, but as one integrated conversation with your biofield. The session is never planned. It is always channelled. Because your field knows what it needs, and my role is simply to listen.
